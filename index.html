About Monica

A software engineer from Colombia. A consultant who led Oracle projects across the U.S. and Europe. A founder who built a company serving Fortune 500 brands.

Monica Hernandez came to the United States at 21 with a software engineering degree and a full academic scholarship behind her. She built her career from the ground up — enterprise technology, global consulting, and eventually her own company.

MAS Global, which she founded in 2013, now serves globally recognized brands and connects U.S. enterprises with elite technology talent from Latin America. Monica speaks from that experience — not around it.

About Monica

Built from
the inside out.

Monica's story isn't background. It's the foundation of everything she teaches — and the reason the people in her audience know they're hearing the truth.

01
Medellín, Colombia

Where it started.

Growing up in Medellín, Colombia, Monica learned early that education was the most reliable path forward. She earned a full academic scholarship to study software engineering — a rare opportunity that would change the direction of her life and eventually the lives of others.

Monica Oficina Medellin.jpg
02
The Move

From Texas to the Fortune 500.

At 21, Monica arrived in the United States with her degree and her drive. She began her IT career in Texas, leading custom application development and digital modernization projects. She then joined Oracle as a consultant, traveling across the U.S. and Europe to lead enterprise-scale technology initiatives for Fortune 500 clients.

Over 20+ years, she developed a deep understanding of how large organizations think, decide, and build — knowledge she would use to start something of her own.

Monica in Oracle X.png
03
MAS Global · 2013

A company built on a belief.

In 2013, Monica founded MAS Global — a technology consulting firm built on the belief that elite talent exists everywhere, and that enterprise organizations deserve access to it.

Today, MAS Global serves globally recognized brands, connecting U.S. businesses with exceptional technology professionals from Colombia and across Latin America.

LOGO MAS GLOBAL AZUL.png
04
The Scholarship

Her story, full circle.

The scholarship that changed Monica's life at 18 now has a name attached to it.

The MAS Global–EAFIT Scholarship was created to give the next generation of Colombian engineers the same transformational opportunity Monica received — while fueling U.S. technology teams with world-class talent.

Monica award in EAFIT.jpg
05
Beyond the Company

Platform, podcast, and purpose.

Monica is the host of the MAS Impact Podcast, where she interviews technology and business leaders about innovation, inclusion, and purpose-driven leadership.

She is also the founder of the MAS Future Foundation, dedicated to upskilling the next generation of women and Latinos entering the technology industry.
